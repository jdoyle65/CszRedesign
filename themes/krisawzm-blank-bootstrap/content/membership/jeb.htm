Journal of Experimental Biology:

If you wish to order the Journal of Experimental Biology, at member rates, go to the web site of the Company of Biologists http://jeb.biologists.org/. Follow their instructions for ordering as a society member. When asked for society and membership ID, indicate Canadian Society of Zoologists, and as ID use the number from the receipt you receive from the Treasurer after you pay your dues. Do not send forms or payment to the CSZ Treasurer. Deal directly with the Company of Biologists. News.
