Membership Categories

  • Ordinary: Any person interested in the study of zoology is eligible to apply for ordinary membership.
  • Student: Any person who is interested in the study of zoology and is a student at a university.
  • Post-doctoral: Any person, within 5 years after receiving a Ph. D., who is interested in the study of zoology and is occupying a position which could normally be considered that of a post-doctoral fellow.
  • Emeritus: Any ordinary member in good standing may, upon retirement from a professional position, become an emeritus member.
  • Associate: A person interested in the field of zoology but who does not hold a research position in a university or a government or industrial laboratory and is not a graduate student. Upon application to the Executive Committee of Council, institutions (libraries, natural history societies) may be granted associate membership.
  • Two-year memberships can be purchased at a reduced rate.

Membership application procedure

Membership Payment by Mail

1. Download a Membership Form (PDF).

  • To download a free Adobe Acrobat Reader for PDF files, click here.

2. General Instructions for Completing the Membership Form

  • Provide full identification information. Please use your institutional address if possible.
  • Choose the correct Membership Category and enter the appropriate dues into the space provided.
  • Identify which section (or sections) you wish to be affiliated with (Sections within the Society have been established for the purpose of furthering the study of a particular area within the field of zoology. Membership in sections is free and you may be a member of more than one section if you wish).
  • Indicate any donations you wish to make to the Zoological Education Trust. The Zoological Education Trust is a registered charity (BN 88756 3864 RR0001) and a receipt for income tax purposes will be issued.
  • Add up the total amount owing.
  • Indicate whether you are paying by cheque or credit card.
  • If paying by cheque, make the cheque out to the Canadian Society of Zoologists.
  • If paying by credit card, please provide all the information requested, including credit card number, expiry date, name of the card holder, and your signature.

3. Send the form and payment (cheque or credit card information) by regular mail to theTreasurer.
