  • Guggenheim Fellow 1959
  • Vice-President, International Association Pure and Applied Limnology 1962
  • President American Fisheries Society 1966
  • President American Institute Fishery Research Biologists 1972
  • Founding Member 1961
  • Councillor 1961-1965
  • President 1966-1967
  • Honorary Member 1974
  • Fry Medal established 1974
  • Medal of the British Empire 1944
  • Fellow Royal Society of Canada 1948
  • Wildlife Society of America, best publication in fisheries biology 1950
  • Flavelle Medal 1962
