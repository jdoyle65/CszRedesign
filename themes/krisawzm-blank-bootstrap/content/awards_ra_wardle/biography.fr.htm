Robert A. Wardle

Biographie

  • Born 1890 Clifton, England

  • Died January 2, 1974 Winnipeg

  • BSc. Zoology, Royal College of Science, University of London 1912

  • MSc. Royal College of Science 1914

  • Manchester Special Lecturer in Zoology 1918-1928

  • British Cotton Growers Association Research Investigator Sudan 1923-24

  • University of Manitoba Head Department of Zoology 1928-1958

  • United College (now University of Winnipeg) Zoology Department 1958-1962

  • Research on entomology and parasitology, particularly tapeworms. Several books including the 780 page "The Zoology of Tapeworms" co-authored with J. A. McLeod 1952. This book was updated by "Advances in the Zoology of tapeworms, 1950-1970" co-authored with J. A. McLeod and S. Radinovsky which was published shortly after his death.

    • SCZ

    • R. A. Wardle Award established 1974
