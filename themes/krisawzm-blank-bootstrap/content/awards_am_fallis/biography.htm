Biography

  • BA University of Toronto 1932
  • PhD University of Toronto 1937
  • Head, Departments of Parasitology, Ontario Research Foundation and School of Hygiene, University of Toronto
  • President Royal Canadian Institute
  • President Ontario Society of Biologists
  • President American Society of Parasitologists
  • Research parasitology especially blood protozoa of birds

CSZ

  • First Wardle lecturer 1975. Address "Is Socrates Right?"
  • Honorary Life Membership Parasitology Section 1980
  • Honorary Member 1981
  • President Organizing Committee ICOPA-V Toronto 1982

Honors

  • Fellow of the Royal Society of Canada
  • Honorary Member Canadian Society of Tropical Medicine
