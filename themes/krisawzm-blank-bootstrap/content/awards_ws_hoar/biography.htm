William S. Hoar

Biography

  • Born Moncton, New Brunswick
  • Trained University of Western Ontario and Boston Univesity
  • University of New Brunswick faculty member
  • University of British Columbia faculty member and Head, 1945-1979
  • Editor Canadian Journal of Zoology
  • Research physiology of fish; numerous publications including the text General and Comparative Physiology, Prentice-Hall, Englewood Cliffs, N.J., 815 pp, 1966
  • Chairman Organizing Committee Sixth International Congress of Comparative Endocrinology Banff, June 1971

CSZ

  • Founding member 1961
  • Councillor 1961-1962
  • Secretary/Treasurer 1962-1963
  • Second Vice-President 1963-1964
  • First F.E.J. Fry medal 1974. Address "Smolt transformation: evolution, behavior and physiology"
  • Hoar Award renamed to honour him 1978
  • Honorary member 1984

Honors

  • Fellow Royal Society of Canada
  • Shinkishi Hatai Medal Pacific Science Congress 1991
