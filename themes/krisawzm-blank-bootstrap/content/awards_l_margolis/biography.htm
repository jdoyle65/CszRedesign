Leo Margolis

Biography

  • Born Montreal December 18, 1927
  • Died Vancouver January 13, 1997
  • B.Sc. McGill University 1948
  • M.Sc. McGill University 1950
  • Ph.D. McGill University 1952 with T.W.M. Cameron
  • Pacific Biological Station, Nanaimo scientist 1952-1997
  • Associate Editor Canadian Journal of Zoology 1971-1981
  • Research- Parasitology of fishes and marine mammals and its application in fisheries management

CSZ

  • Wardle lecturer 1982; Address "Pacific Salmon and their Parasites: a Century of Study"
  • Chair Parasitology Section 1977-1978
  • President 1990-1991
  • Leo Margolis Scholarship named

Honors

  • Fellow of the Royal Society 1975
  • Officer of the Order of Canada 1990
  • Honorary Member Atlantic Canada Society of Parasitologists 1992
  • Honorary Member British Society of Parasitology 1994
  • Gold Medal Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada 1995
  • Distinguished Service Award American Society of Parasitologists 1995
