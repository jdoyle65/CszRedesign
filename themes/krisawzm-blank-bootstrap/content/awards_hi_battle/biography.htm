Helen I. Battle

Biography

  • Born August 31, 1903 London, Ontario
  • Died June 17, 1994
  • B.A. Honours Biology University of Western Ontario 1923
  • M.A. University of Western Ontario 1924
  • Ph.D. University of Toronto 1928. Supervisor A.G. Huntsman
  • University of Western Ontario faculty member 1921-1967
  • Acting Head Department of Zoology 1956-1958
  • Research on the embryology of fish, much of it done at the Atlantic Biological Station, St. Andrews, N.B.

CSZ

  • Founding member and Vice-President 1961
  • President 1962-1963. Presidential address "Zoology in Canada inretrospect"
  • Honorary Member 1967
  • F. E. J. Fry Medal 1977. Address "A saga of zoology in Canada"
  • Helen Battle Award named 1991

Honors

  • Canadian Centennial Medal 1967
  • Honorary Doctorate Carleton University 1971
  • Honorary Doctorate University of Western Ontario 1971
  • J.C.B. Grant Award of the Canadian Association of Anatomists
